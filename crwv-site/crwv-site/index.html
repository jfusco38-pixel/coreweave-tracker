Bull case Lynch

"What does the company do? Why might this stock work?"

  • One sentence: CRWV runs a GPU-cloud business that rents NVIDIA-based AI compute to hyperscalers and AI labs.
  • Demand visibility is unusually high. RPO grew from $15.1B to $60.7B in one year — that's contracted future revenue.
    As of December 31, 2025, we had $60.7 billion of remaining performance obligations … CRWV 10-K, Item 1
  • Multi-year contracts: Meta committed up to $14.2B through Dec 2031, OpenAI up to $11.9B through 2031.
  • Revenue scale-up is real: $0.23B → $1.9B → $5.1B over three years. Triple-digit growth at multi-billion-dollar scale is rare.
    Revenue for the year ended December 31, 2025 increased by $3.2 billion, or 168% CRWV 10-K, MD&A
  • Why the market might be missing it: Headline net loss of $1.2B looks scary, but if RPO converts on schedule the GAAP losses compress as data-center capex matches depreciation.
